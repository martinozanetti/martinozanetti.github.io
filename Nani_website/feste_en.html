Parties in farmhouse!

Italian version

Almost every year in autumn ...
Next edition: 4-6 October 2024!

LOCATION
Farmhouse "Sepiole di Nani", old farmstead from 1600 at 1420 mslm, surrounded by meadows and woods, reachable from Bagolino by car (partially unpaved consortium road, about 10 parking spaces, to be reserved because you have to report the license plates to the consortium) or on foot on the same road (about 2/3h) for those arriving by public transports at the village. The farmhouse is not modernized but is in tidy and spacious: this means that it can be cold and humid especially if it rains.

WHEN
Friday, October 4 through Sunday, October 6. The heart of the festival will be Saturday afternoon evening, with music and warm dinner in the barn.

COMFORT
The farmstead has walls, floor and roof. There is a (cold, 8 °C) drinking water fountain. There will be no hot water except for cooking. Electricity will be provided by a small generator set mainly for music and main lights, and batteries for secondary lights. Any additional electronic devices (cell phones and headlamps) will hardly be able to be charged (with appropriate plug multipliers, cell phones can be charged when the generator set goes). There is no bathroom, but rather a convenient and comfortable two-person latrine.

OVERNIGHT STAY
There is plenty of room to sleep in the farmhouse: about 20/25 places in a room with windows, more comfortable and less spiffy; countless other places in the barn, colder and spiffier. There is then room for about 7 tents in the surrounding meadow. To be brought everything necessary for sleeping (mattress, heavy sleeping bag, pillows, wool socks, hat, earplugs, etc.).

MEALS
The organization will think about feeding you with plenty of good food, and an expense contribution of 15€ will be asked. As alcohol, about 25 liters of red wine is included, but nothing else: the amounts are too subjective, so we ask you to arrange to bring what you estimate to drink. Personal bib, glass and cutlery to be brought. Any tupperware highly recommended to take away any leftovers.

HOW TO GET THERE FROM BAGOLINO
OPTION 1:
Route from Bagolino , which can be covered by car in about 20 minutes (if it is not freezing or too wet), with a pass for the consortium road, or on foot in about 2h30 (arriving in Bagolino by bus or parking the car in the car park where the trail starts). (Opening from the mobile phone, to go to Google Maps click the dotted box in the top right-hand corner).

OPTION 2 (to be verified):
Route from Ponte Dasarè, which can only be covered on foot in about 1h10. There should be space to park a few cars at the starting point of the trail.

Things to Bring

This list is written for the worst conditions of rain and cold, but generally, the situation should be more comfortable than that.

LIVING (warm clothing)
  • Long pants
  • Boots
  • Sweater/fleece
  • Jacket
  • Raincoat and rain pants if you have them
  • Gloves
  • Hat
SLEEPING
  • Sleeping mat (pillow if you want)
  • Heavy sleeping bag (comfort at 5°C, or add layers)
  • Earplugs, eye mask
  • Warm socks
  • Tarp to put underneath if you have it (to protect against dust/dirt/moisture)
EATING
  • Mess kit or tupperware (for possible leftovers)
  • Cutlery
  • Cup
  • Water bottle/thermos
  • Large coffee maker if you have it
DRINKING (alcohol)
  • Bring something to share, thinking about how much and what you plan to drink.
OTHER
  • Umbrella if you want
  • Headlamp (or regular flashlight)
  • Games
